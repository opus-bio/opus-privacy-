Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

1. Overview

OPUS is an iOS application that tracks your biological signals and provides personalized health insights. Your privacy is our highest priority.

Our core principle: Most of your data stays on your device. When you use OPUS Pro features such as Cloud Skin Analysis, selected data is securely transmitted to our servers for processing, as described below.

2. Data We Collect

a) Apple Health (HealthKit) Data

  • Sleep duration and sleep stages
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Resting heart rate
  • Blood oxygen saturation (SpO2)
  • Daily step count and activity data

b) Camera Data

  • Skin scan photos (via front camera)
  • Photos are encrypted and stored on your device
  • If you use OPUS Pro Cloud Analysis, photos are securely sent to our backend for AI-powered analysis (see Section 3)

c) Manually Entered Data

  • Caffeine intake
  • Alcohol intake
  • Water intake (hydration)
  • Profile information (name, age, gender — optional)
  • Menstrual cycle tracking (optional)

3. Cloud Analysis (OPUS Pro)

OPUS Pro subscribers may use the Cloud Skin Analysis feature. When you use this feature:

  • Your skin scan photos are securely transmitted to our backend server hosted on Railway (railway.com)
  • Our server sends the images to the OpenAI API (GPT-4o) for AI-powered skin analysis
  • The analysis results are returned to your device
  • Photos sent for cloud analysis are processed in real time and are not permanently stored on our servers or OpenAI's servers
  • OpenAI processes the data in accordance with their API data usage policy — API data is not used to train their models
You are always in control: Cloud Analysis is an opt-in feature. You can use OPUS without ever sending photos to the cloud. On-device analysis remains available for all users.

4. Data Synchronization

OPUS uses Supabase (supabase.com) for optional cloud synchronization:

  • If you create an account or enable sync, your profile data and health summaries may be stored in Supabase's cloud infrastructure
  • Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS) and stored securely
  • Supabase servers are hosted on AWS infrastructure
  • You can use OPUS without creating an account — sync is entirely optional
  • You may delete your synced data at any time (see Section 9)

5. How We Use Your Data

  • Calculating your personal biological baseline
  • Determining daily mode (Rest / Steady / Thrive)
  • Correlation and pattern analysis
  • Personalized insights and recommendations
OPUS is a health tracking application, not a medical device. Information provided does not replace medical diagnosis, evaluation, or treatment.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Local data is processed and stored on your device
  • iCloud synchronization is optional and entirely under your control
  • When iCloud is used, data is protected by Apple's encryption infrastructure
  • Skin scan photos are stored encrypted on device
  • Cloud-synced data (via Supabase) is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Cloud Analysis photos (via Railway/OpenAI) are processed in real time and not permanently stored

7. Third-Party Services

OPUS integrates with the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API (GPT-4o): Used for OPUS Pro Cloud Skin Analysis. Photos are sent via our backend for AI analysis. OpenAI does not use API data to train models
  • Supabase: Used for optional account and data synchronization. Hosted on AWS infrastructure
  • Railway: Hosts our backend server that processes Cloud Analysis requests
  • Apple HealthKit: Data is protected in compliance with Apple's guidelines

We do not:

  • Transmit data to advertising networks
  • Sell or trade your data
  • Share personal health data with analytics services for marketing purposes

8. Advertising

OPUS does not contain advertising and does not collect data for advertising purposes.

9. Your Rights

  • Data deletion: Permanently delete all your local data via Settings > Delete All Data
  • Account deletion: If you created an account, you can request full deletion of your cloud data and account by contacting us at opusbio.hello@gmail.com
  • Permission management: Enable or disable HealthKit and camera permissions at any time through iOS Settings
  • Subscription management: Managed through Apple Settings > Subscriptions

10. Children's Privacy

OPUS is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13.

11. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated. Significant changes will be communicated through the application.

12. Contact

opusbio.hello@gmail.com

2. 収集するデータ

a) Apple ヘルスケア（HealthKit）データ

  • 睡眠時間と睡眠ステージ

3. クラウド分析（OPUS Pro）

OPUS Proサブスクリプションユーザーはクラウド肌分析機能を利用できます。この機能を使用する際：

  • 肌スキャン写真はRailway（railway.com）でホストされている当社のバックエンドサーバーに安全に送信されます
  • 当社のサーバーが画像をAIによる肌分析のためOpenAI API（GPT-4o）に送信します
  • 分析結果がお客様のデバイスに返されます
  • クラウド分析用に送信された写真はリアルタイムで処理され、当社のサーバーやOpenAIのサーバーに永続的に保存されることはありません
  • OpenAIはAPIデータ使用ポリシーに従ってデータを処理します — APIデータはモデルのトレーニングに使用されません
常にお客様が管理：クラウド分析はオプトイン機能です。写真をクラウドに送信せずにOPUSを使用できます。

4. データ同期

OPUSはオプションのクラウド同期にSupabase（supabase.com）を使用します：

  • アカウントを作成するか同期を有効にすると、プロフィールデータと健康サマリーがSupabaseのクラウドインフラに保存される場合があります
  • データは暗号化された接続（TLS）で送信され、安全に保存されます
  • SupabaseサーバーはAWSインフラでホストされています
  • アカウントを作成せずにOPUSを使用できます — 同期は完全にオプションです
